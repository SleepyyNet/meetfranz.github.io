Work at Franz

Customer Happiness Kaiseress/Kaiser – Internship

Franz, one of Vienna's most exciting Startups, is looking for a Customer Happiness Kaiseress/Kaiser – Intern for 10-20 hours a week. This internship is for a duration of up to 3 months with a potential for full time employment position afterwards.

We take customer service very serious. Every happy customer helps us to grow even faster which makes this position such an essential part of Franz’s success.


Responsibilities

  • Respond to general and technical customer questions on Twitter, ZenDesk, email in a timely manner.
  • Build our Support-FAQ platform based on regular requests.
  • Identify, reproduce and document bugs based on customer feedback.
  • Help our product and development team to define priorities.
  • Make people feel good about using Franz.

Requirements

  • You are outgoing with a great personality, people- and excellent communication skills.
  • You are able to understand technical problems and can report to development teams.
  • You constantly want to achieve the best results, learn from previous experiences and want to share what you have learned.
  • You love to communicate using messenger platforms.
  • You want to learn how Franz works in depth on Windows, Mac & Linux.
  • Proficient in spoken and written English.
  • Be able to work from the Franz HQ in Vienna.
  • You are at least 18 years old.

What would be nice

  • On top of English, you speak (and are able to write) Portuguese, Japanese, French, or any other language.
  • Experience in customer support and startup environments.

What we can offer

Franz is one of the most exciting startups Vienna has to offer. Every day we are facing crucial challenges that help us grow as a team as well. Dive into the Austrian startup community, learn about growing a startup and help us make better decisions. We are an experienced team and would love to share our knowledge with you.

This 3 month internship does not necessarily end after 3 months – we are always looking for bright, creative and solution oriented minds and you may be offered a key position in our growing team afterwards.

This internship is unpaid but if we decide to hire you permanently we will offer a small signing bonus.

If you believe that this is the right position for you then please apply with a short mail telling us what's your background and why you want to work for Franz. Please attach a short FAQ text about how to add and remove a service from Franz. We will only look at applications with a written FAQ example.

Franz is made in Vienna, Austria with lots of and schnitzelburgers by Stefan Malzner and Jonathan Burke

Stefan Malzner is a Vienna based graphic designer and burger lover.

He started his career at the young age of 14 by founding his first startup Global Artists, a community for digital designers and photographers.

After his graphic design apprenticeship he worked as a digital designer for companies like Barclays, Deutsche Bank, Shell, Volkswagen and on the weekends he toured as a DJ and VJ all over Europe.

For the last couple of years he has been on a mission to find the best burger recipe and founded Franz, Horst, Grete, Bloodirony Games and Indiegame.at.

Drop Stefan a line @smalzner

Jonathan Burke is a technology & business consultant.

Hacking on bulletin boards and the relatively early internet through to building websites got him producing e-commerce systems and web-apps for large companies taking their first steps online. This all helped him become one of the founding members of a startup called Toolstation, which was grown into a sector leading international retailer - leveraging the internet to run it's business from the ground up.

With that business sold to a UK top-100 stock market listed company, he moved to Vienna on a mission to build more ideas into more companies.
