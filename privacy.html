Franz Privacy Statement

Good to see you around here!

Franz – a free messaging app for Slack, Facebook Messenger, WhatsApp, Telegram and more. Since your communication is a very personal matter, the safety of your data is very important to us!

1. Definitions

Franz is owned by Stefan Malzner, Vienna - Austria. (referred to as "I", "Me", "Our" or "We"). As a customer of this service you're a "User" or "You" according to this agreement. The App or any services offered by us will be referred to as "Franz", "Franz App" or "Service".

2. Your Data & Messages

Unlike the impression, Franz is very close to a web browser and does not need direct access to the service providers API's. That means Franz does not store any of your login credentials on your computer or on a cloud service. The only data that is stored are cookies and the browser cache – exactly as it is when using a web browser like Safari or Chrome.

Franz does not store your password nor does it directly access your messaging service's account!

3. Statistics

We do not track how you interact with any of the provided services.

However, in order to build a better image of how our users are interacting with Franz we use Google Analytics to track how people work with Franz and what services are being used.

4. Changes

If there are any changes to this privacy policy, we will post them in the App. You can object to any changes by deleting the App within 21 days. Otherwise you agree to these changes.

5. Questions

If you have any questions, comments or just want to say hi, feel free to write an email to hi@adlk.io
