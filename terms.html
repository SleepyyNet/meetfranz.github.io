Franz is made in Vienna, Austria with lots of and schnitzelburgers by Stefan Malzner and Jonathan Burke

Stefan Malzner is a Vienna based graphic designer and burger lover.

He started his career at the young age of 14 by founding his first startup Global Artists, a community for digital designers and photographers.

After his graphic design apprenticeship he worked as a digital designer for companies like Barclays, Deutsche Bank, Shell, Volkswagen and on the weekends he toured as a DJ and VJ all over Europe.

For the last couple of years he has been on a mission to find the best burger recipe and founded Franz, Horst, Grete, Bloodirony Games and Indiegame.at.

Drop Stefan a line @smalzner

Jonathan Burke is a technology & business consultant.

Hacking on bulletin boards and the relatively early internet through to building websites got him producing e-commerce systems and web-apps for large companies taking their first steps online. This all helped him become one of the founding members of a startup called Toolstation, which was grown into a sector leading international retailer - leveraging the internet to run it's business from the ground up.

With that business sold to a UK top-100 stock market listed company, he moved to Vienna on a mission to build more ideas into more companies.

Drop Jonathan a line @imprecision
